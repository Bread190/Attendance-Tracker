Frequently Asked Questions
Q:How do timesheets work?
A:Timesheets are how employees track their working time. They record when they clock in and out, when they take breaks, and then they submit their timesheets at the end of a pay period.
Q:How many employees’ time can you track?
A:With Attendance Tracker you can track all of your employees’ time through the app. Check out our pricing page for more info on additional features like geofenced clock in, custom timesheet exports, or integrations with payroll providers.
Q:How do I check my timesheet?
A:If you’re a Attendance Tracker user you can check your timesheet by opening the app, navigating to the Attendance tab, and select the pay period you would like to view.
